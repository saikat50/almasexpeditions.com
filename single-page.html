Tour Overview

Experience Guatemala and enjoy the top destinations for international visitors! Enjoy 2 nights at Lake Atitlan and visit the surrounding villages of Santiago, San Juan, and San Pedro. Enjoy a horseback riding tour up to the viewpoint a get a tremendous view of the volcanoes and of the entire crater.

Stay in Antigua for 2 nights and enjoy the beautiful cobblestone streets and colonial architecture. Last but not least, take a short 45min flight and enjoy a day trip to the north of Guatemala and visit the world famous Mayan capital of Tikal, lost deep in the tropical jungle.
